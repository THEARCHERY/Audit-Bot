FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before ordering.

What information do I need to provide?
Just send the direct link(s) to the post(s) or video(s) you want audited. If you have specific competitors you'd like to be compared against, include them — otherwise I'll find the best benchmarks for your niche.
Does my account need to be public?
Yes, the audit needs access to publicly available content and metrics. Private accounts can't be audited. You can set it back to private after.
Do you need my password or login?
Absolutely not. The audit is performed entirely from publicly available data. I will never ask for your password or any private access.
How do I pay?
After you email me, I'll get in touch personally. I'll send over a contract outlining the scope and terms. Once the contract is signed and the agreed payment is received, I'll begin working on your audit.
Can I get a refund?
Since the report requires time and resources, refunds aren't available once started. If the report isn't delivered on time, you'll receive a full refund.
Can I audit a competitor's account?
Yes! As long as the account is public, I can audit any account. Many clients order audits of competitors to understand winning strategies.
